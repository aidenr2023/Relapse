PlayerCam
Sensitivy options located in Player->Camholder

Gun 
weapon spread and firing speed options in Player->Camholder->Gun

Wallrunning 
Can move up and down the face of the wall using "W" or "A" keys (up and down on analog stick)
Player moves in direction of Player momentum
Know issue - player will sometimes move opposite of intended direction  


Controls 

WASD or left analog stick - Movement 
mouse or right analog stick - camera control

RB/R1 or F - dash in direction of movement 
X/A or space bar - Jump in direction of movement 

Right click or Right trigger - shoot
